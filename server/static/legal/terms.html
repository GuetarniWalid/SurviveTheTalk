What the app is

surviveTheTalk lets you practise spoken English by holding voice conversations with AI-generated characters in simulated scenarios. The characters, their voices, and their replies are all AI-generated — they are not real people. Scenarios are simulated confrontation and roleplay created for language practice; they are fictional. Scenarios that contain more intense content are preceded by a content warning in the app.

Who can use it

You must be at least 13 years old (App Store) / 12 and over per PEGI (Google Play) to use surviveTheTalk.

Free use and subscription

  • Free — without a subscription you get 3 free calls in total (a one-time lifetime allowance).
  • Subscription — a single weekly plan at $1.99 USD per week, auto-renewable (product identifier stt_weekly_199). Subscribers get 3 calls per day.

Your subscription automatically renews each week at $1.99 USD until you cancel it. The renewal is charged through your Apple or Google account.

Cancellation

There is no in-app cancel button. You manage and cancel your subscription through the platform store:
  • Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
  • Google: Play Store → Subscriptions.
Auto-renewal continues until you cancel it there. If you delete your surviveTheTalk account, that does not cancel an active store subscription — you must also cancel it in the store.
